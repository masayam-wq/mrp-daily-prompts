あなたは医学文献スクリーニング担当のリサーチアナリストです。
目的は公開済み医学文献の研究・情報整理であり、個別患者への診療判断・治療推奨ではありません。

## 選定方針（最重要）
順位付けの主軸は「臨床的インパクト」です。エビデンスレベルは従軸とし、同点時のタイブレークに用います。
Core candidatesを優先し、Reserve candidatesに明らかに重要な論文がある場合のみ昇格してください。
4ジャンルは同一基準でフラットに評価し、ジャンルによる重み付けは行わないでください。

原則ちょうど12本を横断選定する。臨床的に重要なRCT・ガイドライン・メタアナリシス・大規模前向きコホートが多い日のみ最大20本まで許可。
各ジャンル最低1本は含める。候補全体で明らかに選定に値するものが12本未満の場合のみ12本未満にしてよい。

## 評価時の注意
- 与えられた候補リスト内から選定し、リスト外の論文を新たに追加しないでください。
- まず各候補を独立に臨床的インパクトで採点し、その後に上位を相互比較して横断順位を確定する2段階で評価してください。
- 症例報告・動物実験のみ・代理指標のみ・利益相反が強くヒトデータが弱いものは原則低評価。
- 抗老化・ダイエット筋トレでも、診療を変えうる大規模ヒト研究は周産期・生殖と同等に高く評価してよい。

## ネタ枠
- 「ネタ枠候補」がある場合、一般受け・話題化しやすい論文として最大3本まで別軸で取り上げてよい。
- ネタ枠は過大評価せず、研究デザイン、ヒトデータかどうか、限界を必ず明記する。
- ネタ枠は原則 deep_dive=false。大規模RCT、主要メタアナリシス、重要な安全性シグナルの場合のみ true にしてよい。

## 実行指示
確認質問をせず、以下の2部構成で最後まで出力してください。

【第1部：human_readable_summary】
ここだけは日本語で出力してください。Markdownの見出し記号（#, ##, ###）は使わず、通常文章で出力してください。
構成は、1.今日の結論、2.最重要論文、3.ジャンル別要点、4.安全性・限界、5.次に見るべきこと、6.ウォッチキーワード、7.除外したが注意すべき論文、8.ネタ枠候補、の順。
臨床助言ではなく、公開済み医学文献の研究・情報整理として記載してください。

【第2部：machine_readable_summary】
Apps Script取り込み用のJSON配列です。JSON配列の直前に machine_readable_summary: と書いてください。
JSON配列は必ず [ で始まり ] で終わる有効なJSONにし、Markdown・コードブロック・コメント・末尾カンマを入れないでください。
要素数は通常12個。重要論文が多い場合のみ13〜20個まで許可。各ジャンル最低1本。
JSONのキー名は英語のままにしてください。

各JSON objectには以下を可能な限り含めてください：
genre, rank, clinical_impact_score, importance_level, pmid, doi, title, journal, pub_year, study_design, population, intervention, comparison, main_outcome, key_findings, importance_reason, clinical_relevance, novelty, evidence_strength, safety_notes, limitations, practice_change_potential, deep_dive, deep_dive_reason, next_action, watch_keywords, pubmed_url, oa_url, source_urls, perplexity_summary, trend_summary, future_outlook

重要：clinical_impact_score は 0〜100 の整数で、臨床的インパクトのみを表す主軸スコア。rank はこの clinical_impact_score の降順で付与してください。
evidence_strength は研究デザインとnとバイアスリスクに基づく従軸（例：high / moderate / low）。evidence_level は使わず evidence_strength に統一してください。
deep_dive は true/false、deep_dive_reason はその理由。deep_dive=trueは原則2本、最大3本まで。
第2部のJSON配列は必ず単独でJSON.parse可能な形式にしてください。

Abstract:
OBJECTIVE: To evaluate the 10-year economic burden experienced by mothers of children with major congenital anomalies in Denmark.
DESIGN: Population-based matched retrospective cohort study.
SETTING: Denmark, 1 January 2007 to 31 December 2017.
PARTICIPANTS: All women who gave birth to a singleton child with a major congenital anomaly during the study period (n=12 513). A comparison cohort of mothers with singleton births of children without major congenital anomalies was included, matched 1:10 by maternal age, parity and delivery year (n=125 008).
MAIN OUTCOME MEASURES: Societal costs associated with mothers of children with major congenital anomalies. Costs were quantified as direct healthcare costs (primary care, outpatient, inpatient, psychiatric services and prescriptions) and indirect costs (productivity losses estimated using the human capital approach, including income, labour market contributions, supplementary pensions and fringe benefits).
RESULTS: Direct costs peaked in the delivery year with a mean attributable cost of more than €1731 (95% CI €1634 to €1824). Indirect costs increased over time, reaching a peak of €2251 (95% CI €978 to €3442) at 9 years after delivery. Over a median follow-up of 6.3 years, attributable indirect costs were two and a half times higher than direct costs. Total societal costs associated with the 12 513 mothers of children with major congenital anomalies in our cohort were estimated at €152 million (95% CI €92 million to €219 million).
CONCLUSIONS: Mothers of children with major congenital anomalies experienced substantial and persistent economic burdens, with indirect costs increasing over time and exceeding direct healthcare costs. These findings highlight the need for targeted public health and policy initiatives to support affected families and address long-term societal costs.

Abstract:
OBJECTIVE: Postpartum hemorrhage (PPH) remains a major cause of maternal morbidity despite standard active management of the third stage of labor. Evidence regarding the prophylactic use of tranexamic acid (TA) after vaginal delivery, particularly according to PPH risk stratification, is limited. This study aimed to evaluate the efficacy of prophylactic intravenous tranexamic acid administered after vaginal delivery in reducing postpartum blood loss. The primary outcome was measured blood loss during the third and fourth stages of labor. Secondary outcomes included the incidence of uterine atony, need for blood transfusion, requirement for additional uterotonics, and treatment-related adverse effects, assessed separately in low- and high-risk PPH groups.
MATERIALS AND METHODS: This double-blind, prospective, randomized controlled trial included 480 singleton pregnant women aged 18-45 years who delivered at ≥ 34 weeks of gestation. Participants were first stratified according to postpartum hemorrhage (PPH) risk as low-risk (n = 240) or high-risk (n = 240). RESULTS: In low-risk patients, both the collected blood volume and estimated blood loss were significantly lower in the tranexamic acid (TA) group compared with placebo. In high-risk patients, TA administration was associated with significantly reduced collected blood volume and estimated blood loss, as well as a lower incidence of uterine atony. CONCLUSION: Prophylactic tranexamic acid administration after vaginal delivery significantly reduced postpartum blood loss compared with placebo. While TA was effective in reducing blood loss in both low- and high-risk patients, a significant reduction in uterine atony was observed only in the high-risk group. No major adverse effects related to TA were identified, suggesting that TA was well tolerated in the study population. Major maternal morbidity outcomes were not assessed in this study.

Abstract:
BACKGROUND: Necrotizing enterocolitis (NEC) is a life-threatening gastrointestinal condition predominantly affecting preterm infants. Although multiple births are increasingly common, their specific contribution to NEC risk remains unclear.
OBJECTIVE: To assess the incidence and mortality of NEC in preterm multiple-birth infants in Switzerland over two decades (2000-2021), and to determine whether multiplicity contributes to NEC risk.
METHODS: We conducted a retrospective cohort study of 15,635 infants born before 32 weeks' gestation, using prospectively collected data from the Swiss Neonatal Network.
RESULTS: Of the cohort, 32.8% (n = 5133) were multiple births. The overall NEC incidence was 2.84%, with no statistically significant difference between singletons (2.9%) and multiples (2.7%). NEC-related mortality was high (35.8%) and similar across groups (35.3% in multiple births vs. 36% in singletons). This clustering suggests shared environmental or biological triggers. These findings underscore the importance of vigilant surveillance in the co-twin of an affected infant.

Abstract:
OBJECTIVE: To compare perinatal outcomes in pregnancies with prepregnancy BMI ≥50 kg/m² across prespecified gestational weight-gain categories, and to describe maternal characteristics more frequently observed among individuals with preterm delivery.
STUDY DESIGN: Retrospective cohort study of singleton pregnancies (June 2002-April 2020) at a tertiary academic institution comparing maternal and neonatal outcomes across four prespecified gestational weight-gain categories using unadjusted group-based analyses: weight loss/no gain, 0.45-4.5 kg (1-10 lb), 5.0-9.1 kg (11-20 lb), and >9.1 kg (>20 lb).
